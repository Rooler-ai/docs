## Driver mobile app

The mobile app gives drivers everything they need on the road — loads, navigation, and statements.

<Note>This app is for **drivers only**. Owners don't drive — owners see their drivers in the **business app**.</Note>

**Loads** — drivers see every shipment assigned to them, with live status and expected dates.

**Navigation** — turn-by-turn route planning built for trucks, powered by **Here Technologies**, with live tracking on the map.

**Statements & earnings** — every statement is approved and sent right in the app, with driver statement analytics: total earnings, settlements, escrow and advance balances, and a per-statement breakdown of gross, deductions, and trips.
